VeriLens

Privacy and responsible use

VeriLens is a public demo deployment. Uploaded files may be processed by the public API runtime so the detector can return a result. Do not upload highly sensitive, regulated, or confidential media.

The service applies basic request logging and rate limiting for abuse prevention. It is not positioned as a regulated forensic chain-of-custody product.

For production or enterprise usage, move inference behind your own infrastructure, add durable audit logging, and define explicit retention policies.
